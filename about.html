Our Mission

We believe everyone deserves a hobby that fits their life — not the other way around. StartHobby exists to remove the guesswork from discovering new interests, providing personalised guidance so you can go from curious to confident in days, not months.

Whether you have 30 minutes a week or 10 hours, a tiny apartment or a big backyard, we match you with hobbies that work for your unique situation and guide you every step of the way.

How It Works

Three simple steps to your new hobby

1

Tell us about yourself

Answer quick questions about your time, budget, and interests.

2

Get matched hobbies

Our engine scores hobbies against your preferences and shows the best fits.

3

Follow your personalised plan

Get a day-by-day beginner plan with coaching, tracking, and rewards.
